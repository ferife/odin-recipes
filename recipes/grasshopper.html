Grasshopper

Original Recipe

Basic Facts

  • Prep Time: 5 mins
  • Total Time: 5 mins
  • Servings: 1

Ingredients

  • 0.75 fluid ounce creme de menthe
  • 0.75 fluid ounce white creme de cacao
  • 0.25 fluid ounce heavy cream
  • 1 cup ice

Directions

Step 1

Combine creme de menthe, creme de cacao, cream, and ice in a cocktail shaker. Cover and shake until chilled. Strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
